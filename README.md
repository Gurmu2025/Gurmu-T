#Employee Salary Analysis of X Company 2025
This project examines the salary distribution of workers at X Company, with a focus on staff from diverse professional and educational backgrounds. The goal is to know salary patterns, categorize pay discrepancies, and support evidence-based HR decision-making.
#Table of Contents
#Overview
X Company employs professionals from various educational, cultural, and technical backgrounds. This analysis examines salary ranges by department, role, and education level, as well as gender- or background-based pay gaps, the correlation between experience and salary, and provides insights to inform HR policy and salary structure adjustments.
#Objectives
Study salary distribution across departments and backgrounds.
Detect potential inequalities or trends.
Deliver data-driven insights for HR development and fair reimbursement.
Visualize salary data in an easy-to-understand format.
